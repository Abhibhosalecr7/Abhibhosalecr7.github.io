Projects

Franklin City Business Closure Analysis

Leveraged the Yelp Academic Dataset, Yelp API, and U.S. Census data to analyze over 10,000 businesses in Franklin City. Applied geospatial analysis and demographic enrichment techniques to identify closure trends, economic clusters, and gaps in service availability. Delivered insights through interactive Tableau dashboards tailored for city planners and small business development teams.

Airbnb High Booking Rate Prediction

Developed predictive models using XGBoost, Random Forest, and Logistic Regression to classify listings by high booking rate potential. Conducted feature engineering, including one-hot encoding and TF-IDF vectorization of text features. Achieved 89% accuracy and a 0.91 AUC score, offering actionable recommendations to hosts on optimizing listing features and pricing strategies.

Amtrak Operational Performance Analysis

Utilized SQL to extract and process Amtrak's performance data, focusing on delay frequency, revenue per route, and station-level bottlenecks. Built a Tableau dashboard presented to Amtrak executives, highlighting patterns and areas for efficiency improvements. Contributed to a 10% enhancement in overall service operations through data-driven insights.

Gym Workout Analytics and Insights

Processed over 1,000 workout logs to understand behavior patterns in exercise routines and caloric output. Applied clustering techniques and statistical visualizations in Python to segment members and suggest personalized training paths. Enabled a 20% improvement in member engagement and fitness outcomes.
